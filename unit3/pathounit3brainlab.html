💡 The Core Concept: Contralateral Control

RIGHT BRAIN controls LEFT BODY
LEFT BRAIN controls RIGHT BODY

Motor fibers cross (decussate) at the medulla. This means a right-sided brain bleed causes LEFT-sided weakness. Sensory fibers cross too. The only non-crossers for most people: CN I (smell), and hearing goes to both sides.
